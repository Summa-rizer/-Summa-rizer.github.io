### [](https://probberechts.github.io/hexo-theme-cactus/cactus-light/public/2016/11/01/An-overview-of-all-Markdown-elements/#Blockquotes "Blockquotes")<font _mstmutation="1" _msthash="185042" _msttexthash="8075197">块引用</font>

<font _mstmutation="1" _msthash="849329" _msttexthash="13352079">标签：</font>`&lt;blockquote&gt;`

Markdown使用电子邮件样式**的&gt;**字符进行块引用。最好是硬换行文本并在每行前放置一个&gt;。

：

re>`&gt; This is a blockquote with two paragraphs. Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet,
&gt; consectetuer adipiscing elit. Aliquam hendrerit mi posuere lectus.
&gt; Vestibulum enim wisi, viverra nec, fringilla in, laoreet vitae, risus.
&gt;
&gt; Donec sit amet nisl. Aliquam semper ipsum sit amet velit. Suspendisse
&gt; id sem consectetuer libero luctus adipiscing.
`</pre>

预览：

* * *

> 这是一个包含两段的块引用。Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet， consectetuer adipiscing elit.Aliquam hendrerit mi posuere lectus.Vestibulum enim wisi， viverra nec， fringilla in， laoreet vitae， risus.
> Donec sit amet nisl.Aliquam semper ipsum sit amet velit.Suspendisse id sem consectetuer libero luctus adipiscing.

* * *

Markdown允许您懒惰，并且只将&gt;放在硬包装段落的第一行之前。

：

<p>`&gt; This is a blockquote with two paragraphs. Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet,
consectetuer adipiscing elit. Aliquam hendrerit mi posuere lectus.

id sem consectetuer libero luctus adipiscing.
`</pre>

预览：

* * *

> 这是一个包含两段的块引用。Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet， consectetuer adipiscing elit.Aliquam hendrerit mi posuere lectus.Vestibulum enim wisi， viverra nec， fringilla in， laoreet vitae， risus.
> Donec sit amet nisl.Aliquam semper ipsum sit amet velit.Suspendisse id sem consectetuer libero luctus adipiscing.

That is:

  • Square brackets containing the link identifier (not case sensitive, optionally indented from the left margin using up to three spaces);

  • followed by a colon;

  • followed by one or more spaces (or tabs);

  • followed by the URL for the link;

  • The link URL may, optionally, be surrounded by angle brackets.

  • optionally followed by a title attribute for the link, enclosed in double or single quotes, or enclosed in parentheses.

    The following three link definitions are equivalent:

    Code:

    `[foo]: http://example.com/  "Optional Title Here"
[foo]: http://example.com/  'Optional Title Here'
[foo]: http://example.com/  (Optional Title Here)
[foo]: <http://example.com/>  "Optional Title Here"
`

    Uses an empty set of square brackets, the link text itself is used as the name.

    Code:

    `[Google]: http://google.com/
[Google][]
`
